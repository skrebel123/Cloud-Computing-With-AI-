WIRELESS ROUTER SETUP PHYSICAL
==================================
1. CONNECT THE DC POWER CONNECTOR OF WIRELESS ROUTER
2. RESET THE WIRELESS ROUTER BY INJUCT A PIN AND HOLD ATLEAST 3 SEC
3. CONNECT ETHERNET CABLE (MAIN INTERNET SOURCE COMING FROM SWITCH) IN WIRELESS ROUTER'S
   WAN PORT ( COLOUR IN BLUE) 
4. CONNECT 2ND ETHERNET CABLE IN WIRELESS ROUTER LAN PORT( COLOUR IN BLUE)
*** BOTH ETHERNET CABLE SHOULD BE CONNECTED AT SAME TIME
*** AFTER CONNECTING BOTH CABLE YOUR SYSTEM'S INTERNET WILL BE OFF
5. TYPE    NCPA.CPL  >  RIGHT CLICK ON (ETHERNET NETWORK ( RELTALK R, PCI E)
   PROPERTIES  >  UNTICK AND TICK ( TCP/IPV4)  > PROPERTIES  >
  ( TICK RADIO BUTTON ) OBTAIN AN IP ADDRESS AUTOMATTICAllY IN BOTH OPTION
   >  THEN OK....

*** AFTER NO. SETUP YOUR SYSTEM'S INETRNET WILL BE ON......

6.  RIGHT CLICK ON ETHERNET  >   STATUS  >  DETAILS  
*** YOU CAN SEE IPV4, GATEWAY AND SUBNET MASK WILL SHOW THE SAME IP ADDERSS
    EX- 192.168.0.1

7.  NOW SEARCH THIS IP ADDRESS ON GOOGLE .... YOU WILL BE FORWARDED IN THAT ROUTER LOGIN PAGE
    EX- TP LINK, CP PLUS ETC.

8   NOW SETUP THE WIRELESS ROUTER.....
