VOIP(VOICE OVER INTERNET PROTOCOL)
=====================================
Voice over Internet Protocol (VoIP) is a technology that allows users to make voice calls and other types of communication over the internet instead of using a traditional phone line. VoIP is also known as IP telephony.

[Voice over LTE {LTE stands for Long Term Evolution}(VoLTE) is a mobile network technology that allows users to make voice calls over LTE/4G networks.]

USING INTERNET WE CAN TRANSFER BOTH DATA & VOICE AT A TIME.

An uplink port on a network switch is a port that connects a switch to another device on a different network, such as a router or switch. Uplink ports are similar to ethernet ports, but they are designed to handle faster speeds and higher bandwidth. They also often have special features, like auto-negotiation and flow control, to help ensure reliable connections.
