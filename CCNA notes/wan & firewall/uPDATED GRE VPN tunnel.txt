ROUTER 1 TUNNEL CONFIGURATON:----

Router(config)#int tunnel 0
Router(config-if)#ip address 10.0.12.1 255.255.255.0(/24)
Router(config-if)#tunnel source se 0/1/0
Router(config-if)#tunnel destination 25.0.0.2
Router(config-if)#tunnel mode gre ip
Router(config-if)#exit

ip address 10.0.12.1 255.255.255.0

Testing
========

R1#traceroute 2.2.2.2
Type escape sequence to abort.
Tracing the route to 2.2.2.2

  1   15.0.0.5        1 msec    4 msec    1 msec    
  2   25.0.0.2        2 msec    1 msec    8 msec   

**Traffic is not going through the Tunnel it is taking normal path.


So we have to configure Static Routing to send the traffic through the  VPN Tunnel(GRE)

R1(config)#ip route 2.2.2.2 255.255.255.255 10.0.12.2


R2(config)#ip route 1.1.1.1 255.255.255.255 10.0.12.1
